1. **PDF** `output/dandap_manual.pdf` → PyMuPDF로 `output/qa_pdf/p001~p175.png` (175장)
2. **HTML** `output/dandap_manual.html` → Playwright 브라우저 렌더 → `output/html_render/p001~p175.png` (175장)
